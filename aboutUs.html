About Us

Established in the 1990s, our company has been manufacturing high-quality brass products for over three decades. We take great pride in our work and our products, and are committed to ensuring that every piece that leaves our factory meets our rigorous standards for quality and craftsmanship.

Our product line includes a wide range of brass items, from traditional diya lamps and kalash to modern artwares. We use only the finest materials and employ skilled craftsmen to create beautiful, durable pieces that are sure to enhance any home or place of worship.

At our core, we are a company that values excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction. We believe that our success is directly tied to the satisfaction of our customers, which is why we work tirelessly to deliver exceptional products and service at every stage of the process.

Our Process

At our company, we are committed to delivering the highest quality products to our customers. Our process begins with the design phase, where we work closely with our clients to understand their needs and preferences. Our team of experienced designers then develop custom designs that are tailored to meet these specific requirements.

Once the designs have been approved, our skilled craftsmen begin the manufacturing process. They use traditional techniques and the finest materials to create each piece by hand. Our craftsmen take great care to ensure that each piece is crafted with precision and attention to detail. This results in products that are not only beautiful, but also durable and long-lasting.

After the manufacturing process is complete, we carefully package each piece to ensure that it is protected during transit. We offer doorstep delivery pan India within 7 working days of the order being placed, so you can rest assured that your product will arrive on time and in perfect condition.
